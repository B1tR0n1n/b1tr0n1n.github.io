bit

The Irreducible Unit

A zero or a one. Not data — the quantum of decision. Every complex system reduces to this: a single choice made under constraint.

tron

The Instrument

From Greek τρόν — a suffix denoting instrument or device. Not a passive container of information, but a mechanism that acts on it. Agency through structure.

rōnin

The Unmastered

浪人 — a warrior without a lord. Not a deserter. A discipline carrier whose allegiance is to competence and code, not credential or institution.

A decision-instrument without a master,
operating at the bit level,
still bound by the code.
